About Our College

We are a premier institution with a focus on excellence and innovation. Our canteen offers hygienic and tasty food for all students.

GVP College of Engineering for Women has made concerted efforts to focus its Research & Development activities on expanding frontiers of knowledge and technological developments.

To create a name for itself in R&D activities, the college has initiated several steps including funding for conferences, workshops and computational devices.

The college R&D activities are looked after by Prof. K. Raja Rajeswari, Retd. Professor of Andhra University as Director - R&D of the college.

The faculty and students of the college have published their research results in SCI, SCIE and Scopus indexed journals.

To uplift the image of the college, we are planning to organize various technical events in the campus with external funding from various organizations like SERB, DST, AICTE, DRDO and from professional bodies like IETE, CRSI which helps to update the knowledge of the faculty and students in the recent trends of technological growth.
